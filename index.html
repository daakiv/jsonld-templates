JSON-LD Metadata Templates

This page provides access to the current JSON-LD metadata templates being developed for FAIR and Science-on-Schema.org aligned dataset description.

Purpose: These templates are being refined to support a standardized and reusable metadata structure across multiple dataset modalities, including tabular, raster, and related geospatial data.

Available templates

  • Copernicus raster template
    1984 Tanzania 2m temperature yearly average (Copernicus)
  • SVI tabular template
    Expanded tabular template for Social Vulnerability Index variables
  • PM2.5 template
    Wide/vector geospatial example for PM2.5 concentrations

Notes

Supporting notes and rationale are available here: rationale.md

Review approach

The templates are being reviewed with attention to:

  • Schema.org and Science-on-Schema.org alignment
  • Clear separation of dataset-level and variable-level metadata
  • Consistency across raster, tabular, and future sensor datasets
  • Support for reusable metadata design and Gaia-related data modeling
